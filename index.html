About Me

I was raised on a farm in North Dakota. At an early age, I knew that farming would not be my career choice. At the age of 16 when my peers were buying their first cars, I earned the money to buy an Apple //e computer. It fascinated me so much I learned everything I possibly could, including how the circuitry worked.

I took a longer route than usual to complete my college credentials because I worked while making my way through the educational process. I made my family proud by being the first person in the extended Gustavson family to earn a bachelor's degree.

My professional career since 1992 has taken me from a focus on hardware and C programming to software quality assurance, and in recent years, to web development which is my favorite area of technology!
